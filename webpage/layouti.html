The Great Rome

Overview

Rome, the capital of Italy, is one of the most historically significant and culturally rich cities in the world.Known as the "Eternal City," it has a history spanning over 2,500 years and was the center of the powerful Roman Empire.
Today, Rome is a major global city, famous for its ancient ruins, art, architecture, and cuisine.
